About Me

I'm a web developer, designer and educator who loves to create interactive learning experiences. I've spent the past five years collaborating with museums, science centers, educational publishers, universities and other cultural institutions around the world. As a proud generalist, I enjoy working across disciplines to learn something new. I forgo narrow specialization in favor of mastering the skills needed to realize great ideas; both the physical and virtual space. In the past, I've helped create social learning applications, mobile games, immersive themed environments, wacky interactive gadgets, giant play structures, and countless other odds-and-ends.


Outside of work, I keep myself busy exploring a variety of local and web-based learning opportunities. I've enjoyed courses provided by the fine folks at Columbus Idea Foundry, Fuse Factory, modeLab, Dev Bootcamp, P2PU, Code School, Lynda.com, edX, General Assembly and Codecademy. I've also spent time teaching as an adjunct faculty member with the Columbus College of Art and Design. In my free time, I like to keep active or explore my neighborhood record store. I've been collecting vinyl and deejaying since the age of 17 and have a strong interest in electronic music. You can listen to my music productions and mixtapes through Soundcloud. You can also follow me on Twitter to keep up with the things I do.


Specialties: Directing creative ideas from inception to realization, navigating ambiguity, collaborating with multidisciplinary teams, prototyping, self-directed learning, research and content development, mentorship and teaching, education and game-based learning, building relationships.

Skill Set: Ruby Rails JavaScript jQuery HTML CSS Git Adobe Creative Suite 3D
